George Lucas

The Genius Behind Star Wars

Timeline of Lucas' early life and career:

  • 1944 - Born in Modesto, California, U.S.
  • 1965-1969 - "Saw many inspiring films in class, particularly the visual films coming out of the National Film Board of Canada."
  • 1969 - George Lucas graduated with a bachelor of fine arts in film and then re-enrolled as a graduate student at USC majoring in film production.
  • 1969-1977 - "In 1969, Lucas co-founded the studio American Zoetrope with Coopola"
  • 1971 - Lucas created his own company, Lucas, Ltd.
  • 1973 - Directed American Graffiti
  • 1977 - Lucas directs Star Wars and it becomes a high-grossing film.
  • 1977-1993 - "Lucas worked extensively as a writer and producer, including many Star Wars spinoff made for film, television, and other media...He also acted as story writer and executive producer on all four of the Indiana Jones films, which his colleague and good friend Steven Spielberg directed."
  • 1983 - "Lucas and Tomlinson Holman founded the audio company THX Ltd. The company was formerly owned by Lucasfilm and contains equipment for stereo, digital, and theatrical sound for films, and music. Skywalker Sound and Industrial Light & Magic, are the sound and visual effects subdivisions of Lucasfilm, while Lucasfilm Games, later renamed LucasArts, produces products for the gaming industry."
  • 1993-2012 - "It was announced, in Variety among other sources, that Lucas would be making the prequels."
  • 1999 - The first Star Wars prequel was finished and released as Episode I - The Phantom Menace.
  • 2012 - "Lucas served as executive producer for Red Tails, a war film based on the exploits of the Tuskegee Airmen during World War II."
  • 2012-Present - January 2012 "Lucas announced his retirement from producing large blockbuster films and instead re-focusing his career on smaller, independently budgeted features."
  • 2019 - Lucas met with J.J. Abrams before the latter began writing the script to the sequel trilogy's final film, The Rise of Skywalker, which was released in 2019.

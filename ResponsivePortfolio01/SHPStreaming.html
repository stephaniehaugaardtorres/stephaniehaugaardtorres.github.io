SAFE Worldwide Anti-Poaching Initiatives

Poaching does not affect only elephants and rhinos. It's a business that affects thousands of species at a cost of $20 a year in the illegal wildlife trade, the poaching of our oceans, and the bushmeat trade thru trapping and wire snare use.

Emperor Penguins at Risk from Climate Change

The biggest threat to the emperor penguin population is climate change. If our planet continues to warm up, it will detrimentally change the sea ice and destroy the penguins’ habitat. Without the ice, they cannot continue to successfully reproduce, feed, and thrive. Learn more about how climate change is the biggest threat to their long-term survival.

Golden Monkeys Documentary

The critically endangered golden monkeys are found only in four national parks in the Virunga Mountain ranges, typically near bamboo forested areas. They are highly social primates and their biggest threats are human encroachment on their habitat and climate change.
